Frequently Asked Questions

General FAQs

What is Milklisius?

Milkalicios is a milk delivery service that provides high-quality milk and dairy products directly from farms to customers' homes. We offer a convenient subscription-based model for daily, alternate, or one-time deliveries.

What types of milk products does Milkaliciosoffer?

Milkalicios offers a variety of milk products, including cow milk, buffalo milk, low-fat milk, curd, paneer, and flavored milk.

How does Milkalicios ensure the quality of its milk?

At Milkalicios, we source our milk directly from trusted farms with strict quality control measures. Our milk undergoes rigorous testing to ensure freshness, purity, and nutritional value.

Subscription FAQs

What subscription plans does Milkalicios offer?

Milkalicios offers flexible subscription plans for daily, alternate, or one-time deliveries. Customers can choose the plan that best suits their needs and preferences.

How can I change or cancel my subscription?

Customers can easily manage their subscription settings through their Milkalicios account dashboard. You can update your delivery frequency, change product selections, or cancel your subscription at any time.

Can I pause my subscription temporarily?

Yes, Milkalicios allows customers to pause their subscriptions for a specified period if needed. Simply log in to your account and select the pause option in the subscription settings.
